Notable flags
Rectangle Ring: highest return volume by far
63 units returned across 63 orders — nearly one return per return order. 9.7% of all Rectangle Ring orders were returned. This is the #7 product by revenue but the #1 return by unit count. Likely a fit or expectation issue; worth reviewing product photography and sizing guidance.
Compass Studs & Golf Toggle: small volume, alarming rates
54.5% and 40.0% return rates respectively. Low absolute volume (5–6 returns each) but the rates suggest a product or presentation problem. Worth a closer look before featuring either in 2026 campaigns.
Peak sale days generated the most returns
Apr 10–11 had $12,271 in returns processed — the same days as the two biggest sales days. This suggests some immediate buyer's remorse or order errors happening in the sale rush, not just delayed returns.
Venetian Glass Bee line appears in both return rate lists
Both the necklace (11.4%) and bracelet (15.8%) have elevated return rates. A consistent pattern across a product line suggests a shared product characteristic — worth reviewing whether customers are citing the same reason.
